Becky Peterson

Technical Writer

Localization (l10n)

I currently work as a Technical Writer (Contract) on-site at Google via Iconma Staffing. I work on the Cloud DevRel Localization team creating documentation for internal tools and processes and help codify localization style guidelines with editors.

What is "localization"? In general terms, localized content is translated content. But also, localization helps make content relevant to a specific regional audience.

Working in localization (l10n) has illustrated for me exactly why well-written material matters. I'm sure you've heard the saying, "If you can't explain a concept in simple terms, you don't understand it." As a technical writer, you have to be able to break down complex ideas into simple terms. But also, clearly written material makes for easier translation. Localization helps support accessibility. With a global audience, accessibility in technology can no longer be thought of as a "nice-to-have" or afterthought. Well-written docs matter, for everyone.
