Rebecca Peterson

Soprano

Performing with pianist Glenda Williams at the Seattle San Gennaro Festival, 2018.

Rebecca Peterson's credits include Norma (Clotilde, Norma cover) with Skagit Opera, Don Giovanni (Donna Elvira) with Toronto Summer Opera Workshop, The Student Prince (Princess Margaret, Kathie cover) with Icicle Creek Center for the Arts, and Die Zauberfl√∂te (Erste Dame) with the German Vocal Arts Institute. She has also performed in concert excerpts as Countess Almaviva (Le nozze di Figaro, Seattle Opera Guild previews) and the title role of Anna Bolena (Puget Sound Concert Opera previews). She was honored to perform in concert masterclass for internationally acclaimed soprano Deborah Voigt.

Peterson is a recipient of the Helen Jensen Memorial Award in Voice and an alumna of the OperaWorks Advanced Artist Program. A huge fan of presenting opera to new audiences, she believes opera is for everyone.
