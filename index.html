Hi! My name is Becky Peterson. I am a software engineer and technical writer with a background in project management, localization, and music.

I previously worked at a startup building software demonstrations. As one of the first handful of people hired, I learned and performed whatever task was needed on the job, including voice-over narration, audio recording and engineering, technical writing, content management, and project management for localization projects.

In 2019, I wanted a career change and left my job to study coding full-time. I graduated from Code Fellows with a Certificate in JavaScript and shortly thereafter took a job working as a contracted technical writer for Google. I love the people I work with and as a tech writer for Cloud DevRel Localization, feel fortunate to work on a project that helps support tools used by people all over the globe.
