Who I am

Technical Writer | Opera Singer | Developer

Hello! My name is Rebecca Peterson. I am a technical writer, opera singer, and developer.

Previously, I worked as a producer building software training demonstrations for a Seattle startup.

In 2019, I left my job to study coding full-time. I am a proud Code Fellows graduate (Certificate in JavaScript) and work as a technical writer for Google. I feel fortunate to work on a team of kind, smart, and empathetic individuals to deliver tools used by people all over the world.

I have an extensive background in music and theater (WSU, BA in Music, Bass). I am a proud former Tour Actor & Director (TAD) with Missoula Children's Theatre, and helped provide arts education to thousands of students in over 50 underserved communities across the US. I led hands-on learning experiences for students grades K-12 as a teaching artist for the touring production of The Princess and the Pea.

As an opera singer I've performed in the US, Cananda, and Italy. I've also performed with numerous burlesque and variety shows and love any opportunity to perform opera for new audiences.

You can check out my audio files on SoundCloud, read about recent performances, or download my CV.
