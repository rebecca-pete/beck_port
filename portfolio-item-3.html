Rebecca Peterson

Soprano

Rebecca Peterson with pianist Glenda Williams at the Seattle San Gennaro Festival, 2018.

Rebecca Peterson's credits include Norma (Clotilde, Norma cover) with Skagit Opera, Don Giovanni (Donna Elvira) with Toronto Summer Opera Workshop, and Die Zauberfl√∂te (Erste Dame) with the German Vocal Arts Institute. She has also performed in concert excerpts as Countess Almaviva (Le nozze di Figaro, Seattle Opera Guild previews) and the title role of Anna Bolena (Puget Sound Concert Opera previews). She was honored to perform in concert masterclass for internationally acclaimed soprano Deborah Voigt.

Peterson is a recipient of the Helen Jensen Memorial Award in Voice and an alumna of the of OperaWorks Advanced Artist Program. A huge fan of presenting opera to new audiences, she believes opera is for everyone.

Also a former Tour Actor with Missoula Children's Theatre, Peterson helped provide arts education to thousands of students in over 50 underserved communities across the US. She led hands-on learning experiences for students grades K-12 as a teaching artist for The Princess and the Pea.
